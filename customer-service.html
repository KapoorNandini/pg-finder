Frequently Asked Questions

How can I find a PG?

Simply use our search tool to filter and find PGs near you.

How do I contact PG owners?

Each listing provides a contact button to reach out to owners.

What should I do if I forget my password?

Click on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page and follow the instructions. You'll receive an email with a link to reset your password.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach us via email at support@pgwale.com or call us at +91-XXXXXXXXXX. Our team is available Monday to Saturday, 9 AM - 7 PM.

How do I report a problem with a PG listing?

If you find incorrect information or a fraudulent listing, click on the "Report" button on the listing page or email us at report@pgwale.com with the details..

Can I visit a PG before booking?

Yes! You can schedule a visit by contacting the PG owner directly through our platform or calling the listed phone number.

Payment & Refund FAQs

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept UPI, Debit/Credit Cards, Net Banking, and Wallet Payments like Paytm & Google Pay.

Is my payment secure?

Absolutely! All payments are processed through a secure payment gateway with encryption to protect your details.

Can I get a refund if I cancel my PG booking?

Refund policies vary depending on the PG owner's terms. Please check the cancellation policy on the listing before booking.

Booking & Account FAQs

How do I update my profile information?

Go to "My Account" > "Profile Settings", where you can update your name, phone number, and email address.

How can I list my PG on your website?

If you're a PG owner, click on "List Your PG" on the homepage, fill in the details, and submit. Our team will review and approve your listing within 24-48 hours.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, if you wish to delete your account, go to "Account Settings" > "Delete My Account", or contact us at support@pgwale.com.
